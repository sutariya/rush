ABOUT RUSH

Rush is a focused, no-nonsense browser built on Electron. It does one thing exceptionally well: resolve HNS and ENS domains natively.

Developed by Rahul Sutariya, Rush emerged from the need for a truly private, extension-free way to access the decentralized web.

This is not a full Web3 dApp browser. It’s a precision tool for decentralized domain resolution — and that’s its strength.
