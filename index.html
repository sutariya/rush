About Rush

Rush represents the next evolution in web browsing — engineered exclusively for the decentralized internet.

Our architecture provides native, uncompromising access to decentralized protocols like Handshake (HNS), Ethereum Name Service (ENS), and IPFS.

Built for developers, researchers, and pioneers of Web3, Rush is a lightweight, multi-tab browser that delivers speed and privacy without compromise. No extensions, no middleware, just pure access to the future of the web.
