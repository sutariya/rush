How RUSH Redirect Changes Everything

Traditional domain redirection requires complex DNS configuration, nameservers, and registrar settings. RUSH redirect eliminates all of this:

Traditional Way

  • Requires NS1/NS2 configuration
  • Needs DNS hosting
  • Registrar dependency
  • Propagation delays
  • Complex setup

RUSH Redirect Way

  • Direct from blockchain
  • No DNS hosting needed
  • Registrar independent
  • Instant updates
  • Simple TXT record

IPFS Deployment Revolution

With RUSH Browser v2.1, deploy IPFS websites without any DNS configuration:

  • Direct IPFS Serving: Serve IPFS content directly from blockchain TXT records
  • No Parking Pages: Your IPFS content appears as a full website, not a parking page
  • Instant Updates: Change your IPFS CID in the blockchain record for instant updates
  • Zero DNS Dependency: No nameserver changes, no registrar configuration

Example Blockchain TXT Record:

RUSH=your-domain.com
IPFS=QmYourContentID

That's it! Your domain now redirects and serves IPFS content directly.
